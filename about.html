About Us

Welcome to OnlineBUDOL, your go-to online store for high-quality shirts and pants. We are a team of first-year BSIT students from Western Institute of Technology working together to bring you a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience. This project is part of our ITPE1 (Introduction to Technology and Programming Environment) subject. We would like to express our heartfelt gratitude to our beloved teacher, Ma'am Kystal Sayco, for her incredible way of teaching. Her guidance in teaching us HTML and web development has been invaluable, and she made sure we understood every step of the process. Her passion for teaching and her constant support motivated us to give our best in this project. We will miss her presence and hope she finds endless happiness in everything she does.
